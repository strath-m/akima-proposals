# Overview

Northstar Coffee Co. has a strong product and loyal customer base, but the current website is not doing enough to communicate the quality of the brand or guide visitors toward the right action.

This sprint is designed to create a clearer, sharper, and more conversion-focused website experience that helps customers quickly understand the offer, explore products, and move confidently toward purchase, subscription, or wholesale enquiry.

# Goals

- Clarify the website structure and primary customer pathways
    
- Create a more premium and trustworthy digital presence
    
- Improve the way products, subscriptions, and wholesale options are presented
    
- Reduce friction across the homepage and key conversion pages
    
- Deliver a focused design direction that can be implemented quickly
    

# Why this matters

The website is often the first serious trust point for new customers.

If the experience feels unclear, dated, or difficult to navigate, good traffic can be wasted. A clearer website gives visitors more confidence, makes the offer easier to understand, and creates a stronger foundation for future marketing and growth.
