# Overview

Atlas Studio Supply has a strong product range and a clear opportunity to turn more site visitors into customers.

The current store can do more to communicate product quality, explain key benefits, and guide customers toward confident purchase decisions. The opportunity is to create a clearer, more premium ecommerce experience that reflects the quality of the brand and removes friction from the buying journey.

This project is designed to improve clarity, strengthen trust, and create a more conversion-focused pathway from first impression to purchase.

# Goals

- Improve clarity across the homepage, product pages, and cart pathway
- Create a more premium and trustworthy ecommerce experience
- Help customers understand product value faster
- Reduce friction before add-to-cart and checkout
- Strengthen the website foundation for future product launches and campaigns
