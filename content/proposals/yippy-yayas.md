# Overview

Following our recent discussion, we've put together two options to support the next stage of growth for Yippy Yayas.

As paid advertising begins to drive more traffic to the brand, there is an opportunity to improve how visitors experience the website, better communicate the brand story, and increase the percentage of visitors who convert into customers.

The options below provide both a short-term solution and a longer-term strategic investment.

# Goals

- Improve conversion rates from paid traffic
- Create better message-to-market alignment across campaign journeys
- Strengthen the broader Yippy Yayas website experience
- Improve the brand's online presence and customer journey
- Build a stronger platform for future product releases, campaigns, and advertising investment

# Why this matters

Paid advertising can create traffic, but the website experience needs to convert that attention into action.

A dedicated landing page can improve short-term campaign performance, while a broader website redesign ensures every touchpoint supports the advertising investment and gives visitors a stronger reason to trust, explore, and purchase from Yippy Yayas.

# Payment & terms

- 50% deposit invoice issued on approval
- Remaining balance due on completion
- Prices are in AUD
- Proposal valid until 24 July 2026

# Notes

Given the intention to invest in paid advertising, our recommendation would be to complete the website redesign first.

While a dedicated landing page can improve short-term campaign performance, the current website experience may still create friction once visitors begin exploring the broader brand.

A redesigned website ensures every touchpoint supports the advertising investment and provides a stronger platform for long-term growth. However, if immediate advertising activity is the priority, the landing page option offers a cost-effective way to improve conversions and gather valuable customer insights before committing to a larger redesign.

Should you proceed with the Conversion-Focused Website Redesign, the landing page will be included as part of the project at no additional cost.
