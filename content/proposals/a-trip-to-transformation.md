# Overview

A Trip to Transformation is an impact documentary with one clear goal beyond the screen: get people to do something about it.

Rather than a traditional film website, we're designing a cohesive brand and digital experience that guides people from awareness to understanding to real-world participation.

The outcome is a clear, modern, and scalable digital presence that feels aligned with the film, builds trust with the audience, and gives people a simple, compelling way to engage with the mission.

# Goals

- Create a cohesive and intentional brand aligned with the film's tone and audience
- Build a high-quality digital presence that feels credible and delightful
- Guide the audience from watch → QR scan → clear actionable next steps
- Support key actions such as learn, explore, contribute
- Establish a foundation that can evolve over time

# Why this matters

The audience needs to understand the mission quickly, trust the project, and know what action to take next.

A strong digital experience turns passive awareness into meaningful participation.
