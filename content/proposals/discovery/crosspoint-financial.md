# Overview

Crosspoint Financial is exploring a broader modernisation project across brand, website, lead generation, CRM, automation, AI and customer experience.

The current opportunity is not simply to redesign the brand and website, but to create a clearer and more connected foundation for how the business presents itself, captures leads, tracks enquiries, supports borrowers and scales into a more modern operating model.

This Discovery & Audit is designed to clarify the best path forward before committing to a larger build. We will review the current state of the business, identify the highest-value opportunities, assess the right tools and architecture, and provide a clear phased roadmap for implementation.

# Goals

- Clarify the best brand, domain and positioning direction for Crosspoint.
- Identify the biggest opportunities across website, lead generation, CRM, automation and AI.
- Map the ideal customer journey from first touchpoint through to loan team follow-up.
- Recommend a clean, scalable tech stack that avoids unnecessary tools and disconnected systems.
- Define a phased implementation plan with clear priorities, timelines and investment options.
