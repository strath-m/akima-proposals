# Overview

Meridian Skin Clinic has a strong reputation and a clear opportunity to convert more interest into booked appointments.

The current website can do more to communicate trust, explain treatment pathways, and guide potential clients toward booking a consultation. The opportunity is to create a clearer, more premium digital experience that reflects the quality of the clinic and removes friction from the booking journey.

This project is designed to improve clarity, strengthen credibility, and create a more conversion-focused pathway from first visit to appointment.

# Goals

- Improve clarity across the homepage, treatment pages, and booking pathway
- Create a more premium and trustworthy digital presence
- Help users understand which treatments are right for them
- Reduce friction before booking or enquiry
- Strengthen the website foundation for future marketing campaigns
