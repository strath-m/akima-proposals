# Overview

Harbour Stone Advisory has a strong service offering and a clear opportunity to present itself with more confidence online.

The current website can do more to communicate trust, explain the offer, and guide potential clients toward making an enquiry. The opportunity is to create a sharper digital presence that feels more aligned with the quality of the business and makes the next step clearer for the right prospects.

This project is designed to improve clarity, strengthen credibility, and create a more conversion-focused website experience.

# Goals

* Clarify the website structure and primary user journey
* Improve the way services and value are communicated
* Create a more premium and trustworthy digital presence
* Make the enquiry pathway clearer and easier to follow
* Build a stronger foundation for future marketing and growth

# Why this matters

A website is often the first serious trust point for a prospective client.

If the experience feels unclear, generic, or difficult to navigate, good opportunities can be lost before a conversation even starts. A clearer website helps the right people understand the offer faster, trust the business sooner, and take the next step with more confidence.
